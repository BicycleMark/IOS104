Add a launch screen and app icons (IOS104)

Making your app recognizable to users is an important part of establishing a brand. It starts with your app store listing, continues with your app icons, and finishes with your in-app graphics. This course concentrates on the middle part of that process. It shows you how to use icons and a launch screen to establish a consistent look for your app. You will use an asset catalog to organize your icons and include them in your project. Then you will design a storyboard launch screen and make it responsive so it works on all devices. By the end of the course, you will have an app with an eye-catching icon that looks great on the home screen and at startup.

Upon completion you will be able to:

  • Configure an iOS app with all the required and optional icons
  • Create a responsive storyboard launch screen for your iOS app
